Welcome to Hangman!

The classic word guessing game.

How to Play

6 wrong guesses and you lose! Each game has a unique 4-digit ID and you can join if you know the game you are looking for. Or create a new game and invite your friends simply by sharing the URL of the game! Ongoing games are displayed below. (on mobile) or on the right (on desktop).

Nice-to-Knows:

  • Only the user who created the game can give hints.
  • Any user can participate in any game.
  • Won and Lost games remain in the database, but are no longer displayed.
